Privacy Policy

Apple Speech Recognition

Audio to text for WhatsApp uses the Apple Speech Recognition service to convert the audio in text.

Audio files are sent and processed by the Apple servers to get the most accurate text possible.

The Apple Speech Recognition, for some languages, can also works offline, without sending audio to the servers.

Privacy of your data

Your audio/videos are processed only by the application and the Apple servers, we do not keep or store any data.
